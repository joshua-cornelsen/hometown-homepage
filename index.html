Visit Muskegon

From great dining to concerts, festivals & miles of beaches along the coast of Lake Michigan. There is something for the whole family.

What to do in Muskegon

Michigan's Adventure

With over 60 rides, slides & attractions at Michigan's Adventure. There is something for everyone.

Museum of Art

Muskegon Museum of Art is just one of the many museums that is in the Muskegon are for those history buffs to visit.

Heritage Landing

Heritage Landing gives visitors access to the Lakeshore Bike trail path, a walking bridge along with shore fishing. Other events such as festivals and concerts are also held here.

Nightlife

From breweries, to wineries. Muskegon has plenty of options to keep you having fun on your vacation.
